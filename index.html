What is a Robot?

Artificial intelligence or machine intelligence is opposed to the natural intelligence shown by animals. It’s the simulation or mimic by a machine of cognitives functions like learning, solving problems and others. It’s used in various tasks like understanding human speech, reach the highest rank in strategic games, military simulations and many others.

What is the artificial intelligence ?

A robot is a machine that can automatically executing tasks. Robots can be differentiated by their form, they can be human shaped but are mostly designed to fit perfectly with their task. They can also be different depending on the complexity of their programs, the field of their tasks or their autonomy.
