Industrial field

Definition

Industrial robots are composed of arms (oftenly humanoids ones) and were first here to allow a replacement of human employe in risky tasks (nuclear, corrosion) or repetitive ones. In the 70’s, the industrial field had the very first commercialized robot : Unimate. There’s two different types of industrial robots, the mounting robots and the mobil ones that are sometimes using artificial intelligence. Nowadays, industrial robots work in the automotive environment, heavy object handling and precision assembly.
